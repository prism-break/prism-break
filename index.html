Opt out of PRISM, the NSA’s global data surveillance program. Stop reporting your online activities to the American government with these free alternatives to proprietary software.

Proprietary Free alternative Notes

Operating system
  • Apple OS X
  • Google Chrome OS
  • Microsoft Windows
  • Trisquel FSF endorsed, user-friendly GNU/Linux distribution.
  • Debian Popular ethical GNU/Linux distribution.
  • Fedora Fast, stable, powerful GNU/Linux distribution.
  • Linux Mint Comfortable and easy to use GNU/Linux distribution. recommends the nocodecs edition*
  • Tails Live CD/USB designed for privacy.

Apple, Google, and Microsoft are a part of PRISM. Their proprietary operating systems cannot be trusted to safeguard your personal information from the NSA.

This leaves us with two free alternatives: GNU/Linux and the BSD family.

GNU/Linux has a much larger community to help you with the transition. I recommend that you begin your explorations by looking for a GNU/Linux distribution that suits your needs.

Why not Ubuntu? Ubuntu is a poor choice because it contains spyware.

Web browser
  • Apple Safari
  • Google Chrome
  • Microsoft Internet Explorer
  • Tor Browser Bundle Encrypted, anonymous browsing.
  • GNUzilla IceCat GNU version of Firefox.
  • Mozilla Firefox Open source web browser. recommends non-free addons*

Use a combination of Tor Browser and a free web browser of your choice to the surf the web.

Try to use Tor Browser as often as possible (always is ideal). Browsing is slower with Tor, but far more secure.

Web browser plugins
  • Cryptocat Private, encrypted online conversations.
  • Adblock Edge Block advertisements on the web.
  • Disconnect Stop third-party sites from tracking you.
  • HTTPS Everywhere Encrypts your communications from thousands of websites.
  • Mailvelope OpenPGP encryption for webmail.
  • NoScript Only enable JavaScript, Java, and Flash for sites you trust.

Safeguard your privacy and stop websites from tracking you by installing all of these free plugins in your browser.

Adblock Plus shows "acceptable ads" by default, and uses more memory than Adblock Edge.

Ghostery is a proprietary plugin. Use Disconnect instead.

  • DuckDuckGo Anonymous, unlogged web searches. partly proprietary* usa-hosted

DuckDuckGo provides you with anonymized Bing search results.

Startpage provides you with anonymized Google search results, including images.

Pick your poison.
